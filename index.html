Who Am I?

Hi! My name is Kristina Savova.
Currently I major in Information Technologies (Year 3) and minor in Music at the New Bulgarian University (NBU) in Sofia, Bulgaria. I consider myself very passionate, ambitious and hard-working, when it comes to both the two degrees. But let me tell you more about my musical journey and why I decided to study music in parallel with IT.

When I was 4-5 years old, I started playing the piano, as I used to participate in each classical concert at school. Additionally, I would be part of my elementary school's choir; I started taking proffesional singing lessons earlier this year. Also, I became highly interested in musical composition when I was around 16. Since then, I've been studying the fundamentals of musical softwares such as GarageBand and Logic Pro X.

About the Website...

This website consists of my musical portfolio. What does that mean though?

last but not least, on the Feedback page you can share your feedback with me and other fellow website visitors. :)
